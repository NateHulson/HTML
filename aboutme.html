1.0 About me

Hello, my name is Nathaniel Hulson. I’m a creative person that enjoys spending most of my days playing games(especially fighting games) , drawing characters while creating mood boards for little passion projects of mine and also going out to find new hobbies to learn new skills from. Often at times I’ll set and end goal for a project such as my current projects of possibly learning to try to make a video game of my own, animation series or even a comic book series.
